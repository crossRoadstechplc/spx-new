Who We Are
SPX is an Africa-led strategy, research, implementation, and innovation firm that works at the intersection of energy, agriculture, economic transformation, and digital systems. Based in Ethiopia, SPX combines deep contextual intelligence with rigorous analytical capability and strong field execution to support partners working to advance inclusive and sustainable development across the continent.
SPX operates as an integrated institution—bringing together advisory services, applied research, program delivery, innovation incubation, and enterprise development under one platform. This full-stack approach enables SPX to move seamlessly from insight to design to implementation, ensuring that ideas translate into measurable outcomes on the ground.

An Africa-Led Institution Built for Africa’s Priorities
SPX’s identity is rooted in the belief that Africa’s development challenges and opportunities require institutions that understand the continent’s systems, politics, markets, and people from within. SPX brings an Africa-first perspective to development work: grounded in lived experience, aligned with local realities, and capable of designing solutions that work in practice—not only on paper.
As global development models shift and traditional implementers reduce their presence, SPX represents a new generation of Africa-based partners able to deliver with precision, credibility, and contextual fluency.

What SPX Does
SPX delivers an integrated set of functions that reinforce one another:
Strategic Advisory & Systems Design
Supporting economic transformation strategies, market systems approaches, policy analysis, institutional strengthening, and sector reform.
Research, Data & Evidence
Conducting sector studies, applied research, field assessments, and evaluations that inform decision-making across agriculture, energy, finance, and digital inclusion.
Program Delivery & Field Operations
Designing and delivering multi-stakeholder initiatives involving rural communities, cooperatives, MSMEs, and local institutions—combining operational logistics, community engagement, and technical know-how.
Innovation & Enterprise Development
Incubating and scaling African-born innovations through the Spiral Innovation Club, including digital tools, applied science solutions, circular-economy innovations, and sector-specific enterprise support.
This integrated model allows SPX to deliver solutions that are both technically sound and operationally feasible.

SPX’s Work
SPX’s work spans multiple sectors and modalities, reflecting the firm’s multidisciplinary capability:
Energy-enabled Development
SPX implements the a multi-stakeholder program that supports households, farmers, and micro-enterprises to adopt productive-use technologies powered by clean energy. The initiative integrates solar systems, small-scale processing machinery, circular-economy solutions, and community-level training to enhance income and resilience.
Digital Advisory for Smallholder Farmers
SPX has developed digital platforms that provide smallholder farmers with free agronomic and market advisory services. These systems support improvements in farm productivity, quality management, and market readiness across Ethiopia’s coffee-growing communities.
Applied Science & Circular Economy Innovations
Through the Spiral Innovation Club, SPX has incubated applied scientific solutions, including microbial technologies that accelerate the conversion of agricultural waste into nutrient-rich compost—supporting greener farming systems and reducing environmental impact.
Agriculture, Skills & Coffee Sector Development
SPX has incubated and spun off a national-level coffee training institute offering professional training in roasting, cupping, and barista skills. The institute strengthens Ethiopia’s human capital and supports entry into higher-value specialty coffee markets.
Renewable Energy Operations Support
SPX contributes to renewable energy infrastructure by supporting technical maintenance and operational systems for wind energy assets—strengthening the long-term reliability of clean energy investments.
Research & Sector Analysis
SPX conducts sectoral and market research, including studies of Ethiopia’s financial and banking system, emerging digital payment ecosystems, and agricultural value chains—supporting evidence-based policymaking and private-sector innovation.
Advisory for Commercial Farms
SPX provides operational advisory to commercial farms, integrating agronomy, technology adoption, market linkage development, and system optimization.

Cross-Sector Leadership Experience
SPX’s founder has led and overseen businesses across ecotourism, media, agribusiness, and digital industries.
